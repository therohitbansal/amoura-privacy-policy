Privacy Policy for Amoura

Last Updated: January 18, 2024
App Name: Amoura - Dating & Relationships
Package: com.partnerup.amoura

Welcome to Amoura! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, name, age, gender, preferences
  • Profile Information: Photos, bio, interests, relationship preferences
  • Communication Data: Messages, likes, and interactions with other users

1.2 Camera Access

Purpose: We request camera permission to:

  • Take profile pictures directly within the app
  • Upload photos for your dating profile

Important: We only access your camera when you explicitly choose to take a photo. We do not continuously access your camera, record video, or capture photos without your knowledge.

1.3 Location Access

Purpose: We request location permission to:

  • Show potential matches in your area
  • Enable location-based matching features
  • Provide distance information between users
  • Improve match relevance based on geographical proximity

Important: You can control location sharing in app settings. We only collect location data when the app is in use (not in background). Exact location is never shared with other users - only approximate distance is shown.

1.4 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, OS version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features used, time spent, interactions
  • Technical Data: IP address, crash reports, performance data

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Account Management: Create and maintain your account
  • Matching Services: Suggest compatible matches based on preferences and location
  • Communication: Enable messaging and interaction between users
  • App Improvement: Analyze usage to improve features and user experience
  • Safety & Security: Monitor and prevent fraudulent activities
  • Customer Support: Respond to inquiries and provide assistance
  • Personalization: Customize your app experience

3. Location Data Processing

We process location data for the following specific purposes:

  • Local Matching: To connect you with people in your vicinity
  • Distance Calculation: To show approximate distance to potential matches
  • Geographic Preferences: To respect your location-based preferences
  • Analytics: Aggregated, anonymized location data for service improvement

Location Accuracy: We use approximate location (city/area level) for matching. Precise GPS coordinates are never displayed to other users.

4. Data Storage & Security

  • Storage: Data is stored on secure Firebase servers with encryption
  • Retention: We retain your data as long as your account is active. Deleted accounts are purged within 30 days
  • Security Measures: Industry-standard security protocols, regular security audits
  • Location Data: Location history is anonymized after 30 days

5. Third-Party Services

We use these third-party services that may process your data:

  • Google Firebase: Authentication, database, storage, and analytics
  • Google Sign-In: Secure authentication
  • Google Play Services: App distribution and updates

These services have their own privacy policies. We ensure they comply with data protection standards.

6. Your Rights & Choices

  • Access & Correction: View and update your profile information
  • Location Controls: Enable/disable location sharing in app settings
  • Camera Access: Grant/revoke camera permission in device settings
  • Account Deletion: Permanently delete your account and all associated data
  • Data Export: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Opt-Out: Disable specific data collection features

7. Children's Privacy

Amoura is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If we discover such collection, we will delete it immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via app notification or email. Continued use constitutes acceptance of changes.

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 48 hours.
